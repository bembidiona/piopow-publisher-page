About Piopow:
  • Explosive turn-based roguelite.
  • Destructible terrain.
  • Levels are procedurally generated, so every playthrough is different.
  • A culinary story told with ingame cutscenes, and a bunch of surprises.
  • First campaign is 22 levels divided over 5 worlds, which unlocks the 2nd character's quest.
  • Second campaign is hard, and unlocks the 6th area and the bombastic true ending.
  • Each world has unique mechanics and a distintive theme.
  • Each enemy has a distintive behavior.
  • It's super polished and cute. Aims to be a new little gem in the NES library !
Tech & Numbers
  • It uses an NROM-256 board like Micro Mages, Nebs 'n Debs and the original SMB.
  • 2 playable characters.
  • 16 visually different enemies (and not using palette swaps).
  • 6 collectible items.
  • 8 music tracks + 3 tiny tunes.
  • Supports NTSC and PAL.
  • Accessibility options.
  • 1 secret.
